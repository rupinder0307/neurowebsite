Our Services

We offer comprehensive neuropsychiatric services to help you achieve mental well-being.

Psychiatric Consultation

Initial and follow-up consultations with experienced doctors to assess and manage mental health conditions.

Anxiety and Depression Treatment

Effective treatment options for managing anxiety, depression, and related conditions.

OCD Treatment

Specialized support for Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder to help you break free from compulsions and obsessions.

Bipolar and Sleep Disorders

Comprehensive care for managing bipolar disorder and sleep-related problems for better emotional balance and rest.

Panic and Phobia Management

Strategies for overcoming panic attacks and phobias to restore a sense of control and peace.

Anger and Stress Management

Tools and techniques to manage anger and stress in healthy ways for improved well-being.

Behavioral Disorders

Therapeutic interventions for managing behavioral disorders in both children and adults.

PTSD and Trauma Support

Specialized therapy to support individuals dealing with trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

Memory and Concentration Issues

Solutions and cognitive therapies to improve memory retention and focus.
