When should someone see a neuropsychiatrist?
Individuals should consider seeing a neuropsychiatrist if they experience:
  • Mental health symptoms that are related to or worsened by a known neurological condition.
  • Mental health symptoms following a brain injury.
  • Mental health symptoms that are not responding to traditional psychiatric treatment.
  • A need for cognitive rehabilitation after a neurological event or injury.
    • What is neuropsychiatry?
    Neuropsychiatry bridges the gap between neurology and psychiatry, focusing on mental health problems that have a neurological basis. It examines how brain function influences behavior, mood, and cognitive abilities, especially in individuals with neurological conditions.
